Tuition Fees

Our tuition fees are designed to be affordable while ensuring the highest quality of education:

  • All Artisan courses - 15,000/= per term (5 terms)
  • Short Artisan courses - 25,000/= three (3) months (for those with experience/skill)
  • Certificate courses - 15,000/= per term (3 terms)
  • Diploma courses - 20,000/= per term (6 terms)
