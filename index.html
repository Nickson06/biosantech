About Us

BTTI is a private Technical and Vocational Education Training agency registered in the Republic of Kenya as an International Community Based Health, Development, Research and Training Institution, located in Mamboleo Kisumu City, offering training in Biosanitation Technology; Community Health related courses; Building and construction, plumbing courses. BTTI training involves learners in real life service delivery as workers, to prepare them for the job market.

Biosanitation Technology Training Institute (BTTI) is implementing the Modular Employable Skills (MES) under the Competency Based Education & Training (CBET) Scheme to provide vocational training to earlier school leavers and existing workers to develop skilled manpower for the industry. Under the Modular Employable Skills, training to earlier school leavers and existing workers, especially in the unorganized sectors, are to be trained for employable skills.

We also offer services (construction, installation and servicing(maintenance) of bio sanitation systems to households, communities, schools/institutions and market places for those in need of the service/facility at affordable costs. We provide the optimum and customized solutions made for your organization. It is mandated in a number of business areas including construction of Bio-digesters, general construction, sewerage service management and consultancies on environmental impact assessment/audits among others.
